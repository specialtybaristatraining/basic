Disclosure Policy

Last Updated: May 24, 2024

This website, Specialty Barista Training IO, aims to provide valuable free educational content about barista skills and coffee. To help support the running of this site and the creation of content, we engage in certain forms of monetization.

Advertisements

Specialty Barista Training IO displays advertisements, including those served by Google AdSense. These advertisements are a source of revenue that helps us keep our content free for all users.

Advertisers, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses advertising cookies and how to opt-out in our Privacy Policy.

Affiliate Links

Some of the links on Specialty Barista Training IO may be affiliate links. This means that if you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase of a product or service, we may earn a small commission. This commission comes at no additional cost to you.

We only include affiliate links for products or services that we believe could be valuable or relevant to our audience learning about barista skills and coffee. For example, if we mention a specific piece of equipment (like a grinder or a kettle) or a book, and there's an affiliate program for it, we might use an affiliate link.

The revenue generated from these affiliate links helps us to maintain and improve the site, and continue providing free educational resources.

Your trust is important to us, and we strive to be transparent about these relationships.

Our Commitment

Our primary goal is to provide high-quality, unbiased educational content. While we do use advertising and affiliate links to support the site, our content and recommendations are driven by what we believe is genuinely helpful to our users.

If you have any questions regarding our disclosure policy, please feel free to contact us (see our Privacy Policy for contact details).

Thank you for your support!
